About Me

Hello! My name is Kyle and I am a front-end developer studying at Juno College's Immersive Web Development Bootcamp. With experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, I have a passion to build accessible and responsive code.
As a nurse with years of experience in the healthcare industry, I bring my unique perspective and skills to a web development team. My ability to communicate effectively with clients, attention to detail and passion for learning make me a valuable asset to the team. My experience working in fast-paced, team oriented environments has enabled me to thrive in group settings and contribute positively to the success of the team. Whether working in a team or independently, I am dedicated to producing exceptional work while meeting tight deadlines.
With a passion for problem-solving and a dedication to continuous improvement, I am excited to leverage my diverse background and technical skills to make meaningful contributions to a dynamic and innovative organization. Let's connect and explore potential opportunities further.
